My Story

Hello, I'm Shayeer Emanee.

I'm a first-year student at Macquarie University, undertaking the double degree in Bachelor of Laws and Bachelor of Commerce. I graduated from Girraween High School in 2025 with an ATAR of 92.25, and I'm based in Sydney.

Law and commerce aren't separate worlds to me — they're two sides of the same coin. Understanding both means I can read a contract and read a balance sheet, argue a case and understand the business behind it. That's the kind of lawyer I want to become: technically rigorous, commercially literate, and useful to the people I work with.

Outside of study, I run SAZICOR — my own technical practice covering web development, graphic design, and commercial photography — and have been working as a freelance photographer since 2021. I also held roles as a Lead Programmer, a Student Recruitment Intern at Macquarie, and joined El Jannah's team to fund my degree.

I read constantly, write whenever I can, draw and create in my spare time, and play Dungeons & Dragons when life permits. This website is my way of pulling all of that together in one place — and yes, I designed and coded it myself, from scratch. Every line.
